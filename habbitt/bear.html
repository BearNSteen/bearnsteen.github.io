Bear

Bear is a formidable member of the prestigious Bear Knights, standing tall at 7 feet. Clad in custom-fitted armor that gleams with a mix of polished steel and gold accents, he cuts an imposing figure on and off the battlefield.

Despite his martial appearance, Bear's eyes hold a gentle wisdom. His deep, rumbling voice commands respect, yet often surprises others with its eloquence. The insignia of the Bear Knights is proudly displayed on his breastplate, a symbol of his sworn duty to protect the realm.

Born into a noble line of warrior bears, Bear showed promise from a young age. He was inducted into the Bear Knights at an unusually young age, quickly rising through the ranks due to his combat prowess and unwavering sense of justice.

While his primary duty lies in defending the realm, Bear has not forgotten his connection to nature. He often serves as a mediator between the civilized world and the wild, ensuring that progress does not come at the cost of the natural balance.
