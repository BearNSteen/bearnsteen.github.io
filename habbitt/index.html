About

Creatures of Habbitt is a rhythm-infused RPG adventure where every beat counts and every step unveils a new mystery! Dive into a world where music isn't just background noise—it's your weapon, your guide, and your heartbeat.

In this quirky realm, you'll:

  • Groove your way through epic battles, timing your moves to developer-crafted tunes that'll have your toes tapping and your enemies dropping!
  • Piece together a world map like no other, with each defeated foe in dungeon depths dropping tiles that slowly reveal the bigger picture. Who knew cartography could be so thrilling?
  • Rub shoulders (or paws, or hooves) with a menagerie of witty animal companions. From the scholarly Bear N. Steen to the chivalrous Donkey Hote, every character is a pun-derful surprise!
  • Unravel a story that's as sharp as it is silly, navigating through layers of deadpan humor, devious deceptions, and intriguing... intrigues. It's a tale that'll keep you guessing, grinning, and occasionally groaning at the puns.

So lace up your dancing shoes, pack your sense of humor, and prepare for a quest that's one part rhythm game, one part world-building adventure, and three parts absolute madness. Habbitt awaits—will you answer the call of the wild beats?
